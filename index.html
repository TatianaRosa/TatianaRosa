About Me

Welcome! I am Tatiana Rosa, a PhD candidate in Economics at the University of Campinas (UNICAMP), Brazil. My main research interests are in macroeconomics, inequality, development economics, and income distribution. I am currently developing research on the relationship between wage inequality and macroeconomic dynamics.

I have experience in teaching, research, and data analysis, and have participated in projects related to labor markets, gender, and economic development.
